Ultrafast photophysics of devices (UPD) group leads a unique research direction to understand ultrafast dynamics of next generation optoelectronic and electronic devices. The research of UPD group is to bridge the gap between fundamental photophysics and novel devices, and to develop next generation technologies to contribute to society.

UPD group research includes three areas:

  • Ultrafast carrier dynamics in situ devices;
  • Next generation device development: QLEDs, IR detectors, solar cells, and FETs.
  • Quantum materials synthesis and novel property characterization.
