Dr. Gao received his M.Sc. and Ph.D. from the University of Alberta, Canada. He has been a postdoctoral fellow at the National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL), Los Alamos National Laboratory (LANL), and Department of Chemistry at University of California, Berkeley.

Dr. Gao research focus to develop next generation technologies of quantum materials and use unique ultrafast photocurrent spectroscopy approaches to understand fundamental device photophysics dynamics. Hence, his research includes three areas:

  1. Ultrafast photophysics in situ devices
  2. Next generation device development
  3. Quantum materials synthesis and novel property characterization

Dr. Gao's research is highly interdisciplinary and at the interface of Physics, Materials Sciences and Engineering, Chemistry, and Electrical Engineering.
